Custom Powered Speaker

My adventure into amplifier/speaker design!

Ever since I can remember, I've been captivated by loudspeakers. The ability to transform any space into a personal, high-fidelity soundstage has always been a source of immense joy. I still remember the reactions of my friends when they realized the movie theater quality of sound was thanks to a single compact, off-the-shelf loudspeaker in my apartment. This simple setup brought me an extraordinary amount of delight, effectively turning my living space into a portable cinema. This passion led me to embark on a project where I designed a custom loudspeaker from scratch.

The goal was to define the performance specifications I wanted from the speaker, select the drivers, design and assemble an amplifier tailored to their specifications, and build a precisely tuned enclosure to house all the components. Currently, the project is in its advanced stages, with all the necessary supplies procured and the circuitry functioning. To document the progress, I've created a detailed tracking document that details each step of the process. This project is a testament to my dedication to achieving exceptional audio quality and my commitment to understanding the intricate art of loudspeaker design.
