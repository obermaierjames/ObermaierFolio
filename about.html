James Obermaier

Acoustic Engineer

Hello! My name is James Obermaier and I'm from Long Island, NY, now living in the Boston area. I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with a self-designed interdisciplinary bachelor's degree focused in acoustic engineering. I have a passion for acoustics and problem solving, and am interested in pursuing roles related to noise and vibration.

I love spending my spare time outdoors hiking, snowboarding, and kayaking with friends. I enjoy working on personal projects, playing guitar, and making music on Ableton. I rarely sit still and like the phrase "work hard, play hard".
