My Adventures in Ableton

I have been playing instruments ever since I could remember. Starting with basic rhythm instruments and the recorder, I eventually made my way to ukulele, tuba, baritone, trombone, etc. I even made my way to taking music theory courses in high school and joining every band I could. My love for music stoked my creativity and I was hooked.

In college I took a number of music courses, completing part of my humanities requirement and a minor focused in music. My courses spanned topics such as theory, music psychology, electronic music composition, and even musical robotics! In my spare time I've dabbled in music production using softwares such as FL Studio and Ableton to play and record my own music. Recently I've been playing a lot of guitar and for fun I post some of what I've made online with my friend Donny. Check out some of my music here at the link to the right!
