Woodworking

My adventures in woodworking

Woodworking has been a relaxing hobby I've participated in since high school. It all started in the woodshop, where I frequently dedicated my free periods and post-school hours to personal projects. Over time, I've crafted an assortment of items, from soup spoons and pens to wood intarsias and sturdy desks. I've also delved into wood burning projects and created paintings using wood as the canvas. Below, you'll find a diverse display of these endeavors. These undertakings have provided me with the chance to employ various tools, including lathes, circular saws, drills, sanders, CNC mills, and wood burners.
